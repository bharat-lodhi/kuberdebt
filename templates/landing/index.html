Our Story

How KuberDebt Began

KuberDebt was founded because we witnessed the suffering of individuals overwhelmed by financial hardship. Following crises like business losses, job loss, or medical emergency, they faced relentless harassment and threats, instead of support. We recognized this as a crisis of dignity and peace of mind.

Debtors needed more than just advice—they needed powerful legal support to resolve the matter. Our mission became: To restore financial status and peace to the individual by amicably resolving disputes. KuberDebt does not promote loan defaults but takes an initiative to resolve and settle the loans. Our service not only helps you settle existing debt but also provides a financial strategy to ensure you don't end up in this situation again.
